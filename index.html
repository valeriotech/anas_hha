Welcome to Ana's Home Care

Providing Affordable Care for Seniors and Individuals with Disabilities

Who We Are

We are a home health agency that provides affordable care for seniors and individuals with disabilities. Our mission is to help our clients live independently in the comfort of their own homes. We offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of our clients and their families. Our caregivers are trained professionals who are dedicated to providing quality care with compassion and respect.

Our Caregivers

Our caregivers are trained professionals who have experience working with seniors and individuals with disabilities. They are compassionate, reliable, and dedicated to providing quality care to our clients. Most are fluent in English and Spanish, allowing them to communicate effectively with clients and their families.

Services Details

Personal Care: Our caregivers provide assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting while maintaining dignity.

Companion Care: Companionship and conversation to reduce isolation and boost mental well-being.

Meal Preparation: We plan and prepare nutritious meals that suit dietary needs and preferences.

Homemaking: Light cleaning and laundry services for a comfortable home environment.

Appointment Companion: We accompany clients to their appointments, providing support.

Respite Care: Flexible, temporary relief for family caregivers who need a break.
