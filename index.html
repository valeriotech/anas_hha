Welcome to Ana's Home Care

Providing Affordable Care for Seniors and Individuals with Disabilities

Who We Are

We are a home health agency that provides affordable care for seniors and individuals with disabilities. Our mission is to help our clients live independently in the comfort of their own homes. We offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of our clients and their families. Our caregivers are trained professionals who are dedicated to providing quality care with compassion and respect. We are committed to making a positive difference in the lives of our clients and their families.

Our Caregivers

Our caregivers are trained professionals who have experience working with seniors and individuals with disabilities. They are compassionate, reliable, and dedicated to providing quality care to our clients. Our caregivers are fluent in English and Spanish, and are able to communicate effectively with our clients and their families. They are committed to making a positive difference in the lives of our clients and their families, and are available to provide care 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Services Details

Personal Care: Our caregivers provide assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting. They are trained to help our clients maintain their personal hygiene and dignity.

Companion Care: Our caregivers provide companionship and conversation to help our clients feel less isolated and more connected to the world around them.

Meal Preparation: Our caregivers help our clients plan and prepare nutritious meals that meet their dietary needs and preferences.

Homemaking: Our caregivers provide light housekeeping and laundry services to help our clients maintain a clean and comfortable living environment.

Transportation: Our caregivers provide transportation to appointments and errands to help our clients stay connected to their community and maintain their independence.

Respite Care: Our caregivers provide temporary relief for family caregivers who need a break from their caregiving responsibilities. We offer flexible scheduling to meet the needs of our clients and their families.
