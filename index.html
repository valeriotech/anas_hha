Detailed Service Descriptions

Personal Care: Our caregivers provide compassionate assistance with daily activities including bathing, dressing, grooming and toileting while always maintaining dignity and privacy.

Companion Care: Meaningful companionship and engaging conversation to reduce isolation, combat loneliness and promote emotional well‑being and mental stimulation.

Meal Preparation: We plan and prepare nutritious, delicious meals that accommodate dietary restrictions, preferences and special nutritional needs.

Light Housekeeping: Light housekeeping, laundry and organization services to maintain a clean, comfortable and safe home environment.

Transportation & Errands: Reliable transportation and companionship to medical appointments, errands and social activities to help maintain independence.

Respite Care: Flexible, temporary relief for family caregivers who need time to rest, work or attend to personal matters while ensuring continuous care for their loved ones.

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the difference between home care and home health?

Home care provides non‑medical assistance such as personal care, companionship and household support, while home health involves skilled nursing or therapy ordered by a physician.

Do you accept private pay, long‑term care insurance or Medicaid?

We accept private pay and many long‑term care insurance plans. We can help you explore your insurance options during your consultation. Medicaid is not currently accepted.

What are your minimum hours per visit?

Our minimum visit duration is typically four hours, though longer visits and live‑in care can be arranged to suit your loved one's needs.

How quickly can services start?

Following the in‑home assessment, services can usually begin within 48 hours. We strive to accommodate urgent requests whenever possible.

Do you offer overnight or live‑in care?

Yes. We provide overnight and live‑in care arrangements to ensure continuous support and safety for clients who need around‑the‑clock assistance.
